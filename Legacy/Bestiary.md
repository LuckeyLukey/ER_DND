# Spirit Jellyfish Ashes
**Level:** 2nd  
**School:** Conjuration  
**Casting Time:** 1 Action  
**Range:** 30 ft  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes  

You summon a spectral jellyfish spirit that aids you in battle.

## Summon
A **Spirit Jellyfish** appears in an unoccupied space within range.

---

## Spirit Jellyfish
*Medium spirit, neutral*

**Speed:** 20 ft, hover  

**STR** 8 (-1)  
**DEX** 12 (+1)  
**CON** 12 (+1)  
**INT** 3 (-4)  
**WIS** 10 (+0)  
**CHA** 5 (-3)  

**Damage Resistances:** poison, necrotic  
**Condition Immunities:** charmed, frightened  
**Senses:** blindsight 30 ft (blind beyond)  
**Languages:** understands your commands  

---

## Actions

### Poison Spit
*Ranged Spell Attack:* +4 to hit, range 30 ft  
Hit: **1d8 poison damage**, and the target must succeed on a CON saving throw or be **poisoned until the end of its next turn**

---

## Traits

### Spirit Companion
Acts immediately after your turn and obeys your verbal commands (no action required).

### Floating Form
Can move through creatures and objects as difficult terrain.

---

# Lone Wolf Ashes
**Level:** 2nd  
**School:** Conjuration  
**Casting Time:** 1 Action  
**Range:** 30 ft  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes  

You summon three spectral wolves that hunt as a pack.

## Summon
Three **Spirit Wolves** appear in unoccupied spaces within range.

---

## Spirit Wolf
*Small spirit, neutral*

**AC:** 13  
**HP:** 11 (2d8 + 2)  
**Speed:** 40 ft  

**STR** 12 (+1)  
**DEX** 15 (+2)  
**CON** 12 (+1)  
**INT** 3 (-4)  
**WIS** 12 (+1)  
**CHA** 6 (-2)  

**Senses:** darkvision 60 ft  
**Languages:** understands your commands  

---

## Actions

### Bite
*Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft  
Hit: **1d6 + 2 piercing damage**

---

## Traits

### Pack Tactics
The wolf has advantage on attack rolls if at least one ally is within 5 ft of the target.

### Spirit Companion
All wolves act on the same initiative (after your turn) and follow your commands.

### Harassing Pack
If at least two wolves hit the same target in a round, that target must succeed on a STR saving throw or be **knocked prone**.

---

## At Higher Levels
- Each wolf gains **+5 HP per slot level above 2nd**  
- At 4th level slot: summon **4 wolves instead of 3**
