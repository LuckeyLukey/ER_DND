# Lone Wolf Ashes
**Level:** 2nd  
**School:** Conjuration  
**Casting Time:** 1 Action  
**Range:** 30 ft  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes  

You summon three spectral wolves that hunt as a pack.

## Summon
Three **Spirit Wolves** appear in unoccupied spaces within range.

---

## Spirit Wolf
*Small spirit, neutral*

**AC:** 13  
**HP:** 11 (2d8 + 2)  
**Speed:** 40 ft  

**STR** 12 (+1)  
**DEX** 15 (+2)  
**CON** 12 (+1)  
**INT** 3 (-4)  
**WIS** 12 (+1)  
**CHA** 6 (-2)  

**Senses:** darkvision 60 ft  
**Languages:** understands your commands  

---

## Actions

### Bite
*Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft  
Hit: **1d6 + 2 piercing damage**

---

## Traits

### Pack Tactics
The wolf has advantage on attack rolls if at least one ally is within 5 ft of the target.

### Spirit Companion
All wolves act on the same initiative (after your turn) and follow your commands.

### Harassing Pack
If at least two wolves hit the same target in a round, that target must succeed on a STR saving throw or be **knocked prone**.

---

## At Higher Levels
- Each wolf gains **+5 HP per slot level above 2nd**  
- At 4th level slot: summon **4 wolves instead of 3**
